Introduction

In2Viewer™ is a technology services provider

In2Viewer™ is technology service provider start-up founded in 2020 in Neptune, New Jersey. Our company mission is to provide a Total Recruitment Lifecycle Solution for the Remote Working Community.

As a minority owned business comprised of a diverse team of unique talent, background and experiences, the three key goals of the In2Viewer™ are:

01.
Lead with Today’s Technology

Provide a truly innovative and custom platform for those seeking and sourcing for partial and fully remote career opportunities leveraging the best-in-class technologies available.

02.
Champion Work/Life Balance

Use our platform to advocate on the importance of flexibility in the workplace to help ensure and enhance job satisfaction for workers as well as help reduce turnover rates for employers.

03.
Deliver Value through Efficiency

Elevate the communication channels between jobseekers and employers in order to eliminate inefficiencies in the recruitment experience for all stakeholders.

How It Works

1

Create your account

Complete the sign-up details page. One submitted, a verification email will be generated with an invitation welcoming you with a link to complete account set-up.

2

Set-up your Profile

Jobseekers are encouraged to populate their profiles with relevant details, most notabably the interview availabilty section which will be displayed publically on your profile for users to view. For companies, the email address provided shall serve as the primary admin’s log-in to Company dashboard. Populate company profile with relevant details for display as the organization company page in the website. Invite recruiters as permitted by subscription to via email join your company to begin posting jobs.

3

Start your Search

Jobseekers and Recruiters begin searching though the In2Viewer™ Jobs and Connections database to match talent and qualifications to open job opportunities.

4

Apply

Jobseekers apply to open jobs by submiting their resume/covers for consideration.

5

Evaluate & Connect

Recruiters receive candidate submissions and will at their discretion: make an offer, message a candidate directly for further discussion, schedule Interview, or decline a candidate for further consideration

6

Interview Scheduling

The recruiter launches the Interview Scheduler window, populates all relevant fields and sends to the Jobseeker for acceptance

7

Acceptance & Calendar Synching

Jobseeker receives the Interview request as pending invite and upon acceptance, the event syncs to the recuiter and jobseekers respective calendars as confirmed.

8

Interview Hosting

Interview is conducted in the manner intended with all relevant parties present.

9

Offer Extension

Upon successful completion of the interview and at their discretion, Recruiters shall compose or attach a formal offer letter along with any supporting documents and send to a prospective candidate for review.

10

Confirmation & Close-out

Jobseeker receives the offer as pending and upon acceptance, the platform immediately records the candidate as hired and the job posting is closed

Quality
Matters to Us

In2Viewer LLC has implemented a quality management system that exists as part of a larger strategy that has established, documented and implemented our processes, quality policies and objectives, whilst satisfying the requirements of ISO 9001:2015.

To achieve this, In2Viewer LLC has adopted the process approach advocated by ISO 9001:2015. Top management has determined the processes required for achieving the intended outputs. By defining four key process-groups and by managing their inputs, activities, controls, outputs and interfaces; we ensure that system effectiveness is established maintained. These key process groups include:
1. Leadership and planning processes.
2. Customer and stakeholder processes.
3. Product/service development processes.
4. Evaluation and improvement processes
